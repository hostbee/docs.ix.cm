## IV. Cookies and Other Tracking Mechanisms

We and our third-party service providers use cookies and similar technologies to track your use of our Site.

### 4.1 Cookies

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers stored on your device. They enable:

- Easier navigation
- Faster login processes
- Activity tracking and analytics
- Online advertising optimization

### 4.2 Disabling Cookies

Most browsers accept cookies by default. You may configure your browser to:

- Block new cookies
- Receive notifications when cookies are set
- Disable cookies entirely

> ⚠️ **Note:** Disabling cookies may limit certain Site features.

### 4.3 Clear GIFs and Pixel Tags

We may use clear GIFs (web beacons) to:

- Track visitor activities
- Manage content
- Compile usage statistics
- Monitor email engagement (open rates, clicks, forwards)

### 4.4 Third-Party Analytics

We use automated tools, including **Google Analytics**, to evaluate Site usage.

- Google's Privacy Policy: [https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
- Opt-out: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### 4.5 Do-Not-Track Signals

Our systems do not currently recognize browser "Do-Not-Track" requests. You may disable tracking by adjusting cookie settings or opting out of targeted advertising.
